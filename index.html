About us

Story behind Pixstory

Love, dreams, happiness, togetherness, friends, family-- life is about everything beautiful. And the most beautiful part? The memories. What can be more delightful than a moment that manages to brighten your day years later. It was the urge to frame those beautiful moments for life that motivated Saikat, an IIT alumnus and Anirban, an IIFT alumnus to start Pixstory. The mundane grind of the corporate world has failed to kill the imagination of these multi-talented individuals, both of whom live their dreams every day.

Pixstory is the solution for your needs to keep the valuable moments of life alive forever. Send us your requirements. Our analysts will get back to you with the optimized solution. The expert photographers across India will cover your event.
